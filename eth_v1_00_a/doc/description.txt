eth_v1_00_a: Eth Version 1.00 Revision A
Minimalistic CoreConnect OPB-Slave Ethernet interface


Introduction:
	Eth is a small ethernet MAC for academic purposes only. 
	Eth is designed for simplicity and modularity, with minimal functionallity.
	Eth is 10 MBit full duplex only.

Status:
	Eth is not completed. Eth completes _some_ tests correctly, but not all.
